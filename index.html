Heroes Sky Fight

You are the last hope of the human race! Pick your hero and start fighting against enemies trying to kill human race! Move your hero to avoid or kill the enemies, save the falling humans, beat the bosses and save the human race. Don't forget to look around, some people might want to help you by leaving med-packs around!

Controls

  • Arrow Left/Right - Moves the hero
  • Space - Shoot
